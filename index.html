I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Dr. Navin Goyal, Dr. Adam Tauman Kalai, Dr. Lester Mackey and Prof. Siddhartha Gadgil (IISC Banglore)

I am currently interested in understanding the limitations and capabilities of recent AI systems such as Large Language Models (LLMs) covering various aspects from reasoning to memorisation. With this, I aim to build better AI systems for diverse problems such as theorem proving, factual question answering and program synthesis.

Keywords: formal theorem proving, analysis and interpretability, search and game playing

I graduated with B.E. (Hons.) in Computer Science from BITS Pilani - Goa Campus, India in 2020. For more details about my background, refer to my CV. If you'd like to chat with me about my work or research in general, feel free to reach out!
